About


Hi! I'm Audrey and I am a full stack web developer and artist. I been making websites for eight years now, since I was ten years old.

I am currently a student at the Kansas City Art Institute, studying Animation. I have taken several design, art, and programming classes in the past that have fueled my love for web development.

In high school I taught a class of 15 middle school students to make websites, and in college I am doing private tutoring on web development. I have built both personal projects and projects for schools I have attended.

In 2010 I started my learning with HTML, CSS, and PHP. Over time, I added some Javascript and jQuery to the mix. I have been learning and utilizing Python to build my websites since 2015, using the framework Django.

Art/Design

I use a lot of the tools from the Adobe Suite when creating my art and design.

I use Adobe Illustrator for creating vector graphics and SVG assets for my websites. I also use it to make design mockups for websites.

Adobe Photoshop is how I create most of my art, ranging from photorealistic digital paintings to small and complex pixel art.

Experience design helps me to create wireframes, mockups, and plans before creating my websites.
